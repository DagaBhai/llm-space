Paris is known worldwide as the City of Light, celebrated for its historic architecture and romantic atmosphere.

French cuisine, from freshly baked baguettes to delicate macarons, is celebrated as a pinnacle of culinary art.

The Louvre Museum houses thousands of years of human art history, including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
High fashion and haute couture have their historic heart in Paris, influencing global style trends for centuries.
The café culture in Paris encourages people to slow down, sip an espresso, and watch the world go by.
Regions like Bordeaux and Champagne are globally renowned for producing some of the finest wines in the world.

French philosophy and the Age of Enlightenment heavily shaped modern concepts of democracy and human rights.

The French concept of "joie de vivre" reflects a deep cultural appreciation for the joys of daily life.
